About Me

Hi! I'm Rachael Chung, a freshman at Carnegie Mellon University studying mechanical engineering and the intersection of arts and technology. I'm interested in interactive story-telling and the ways technology can enhance human experiences.

Before college, I spent years exploring the creative side of things: graphic design, video editing, building social media content. I learned how to tell stories visually, how to make complex ideas feel accessible, and how to communicate in effective ways.

As I now dive into engineering, I'm learning to blend my arts background with my technical experience. Whether I'm designing a mechanism or crafting a visual narrative, I'm always thinking about how to bridge the gap between function and feeling, between technical precision and human connection.

I'm driven by curiosity, energized by challenges, and always looking for ways to combine my interests in unexpected ways. Thanks for stopping by to learn more about my journey!
