About Me

Hi! I'm Rachael, a freshman studying mechanical engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm most interested in interactive storytelling and the border between where engineering and art intersect to create unique human experiences.

I'm excited by combining technical and artistic work. Whether I'm designing a mechanism or crafting a visual story, I'm driven by the challenge of making complex ideas accessible and engaging.
