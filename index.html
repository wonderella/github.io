About Me

Hi! I'm Rachael, a freshman studying mechanical engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. My path to engineering has been anything but traditional; I've spent years developing skills in graphic design, video editing, and social media content creation.

What excites me most is finding ways to bridge creative and technical work. Whether I'm designing a mechanism or crafting a visual story, I'm driven by the challenge of making complex ideas accessible and engaging.
